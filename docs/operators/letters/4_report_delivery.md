### How to Use Your Portal

**Reviewing findings:**
Navigate to the Findings page. Each finding has a severity rating, a plain-language explanation of the risk, and step-by-step remediation instructions specific to your environment.

**Tracking remediation:**
When your team completes a remediation item, click "Mark as Resolved" on that finding and add a brief note describing what was done (e.g., "Enabled MFA for all users via Entra ID"). This keeps the audit trail current and updates your NIST coverage score automatically.

**Sharing with your team:**
The portal URL and password can be shared with your IT administrator or any internal stakeholder who needs to review the findings or track progress.
