| Area | What We Look At |
|------|----------------|
| **Identity & Access** | Multi-factor authentication coverage, guest user exposure, privileged account controls |
| **AI Tool Usage** | Which AI applications (ChatGPT, Copilot, Claude, etc.) have access to your Microsoft 365 data and what permissions they hold |
